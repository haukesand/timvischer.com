In “For ______ ́s sake” I displayed the child-mother relationship of the four big world-religions,
how theyre explained in the original written word. I applied an post Internet art aestheitc
to create an iconography which is easier to access for modern generations. My attempt was to create
a mirror to nowaday religious child education that is far different from how its explained in the holy scriptures.
